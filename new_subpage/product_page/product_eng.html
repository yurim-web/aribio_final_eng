Summary of Clinical Findings

01

Support for cognitive function

Significant improvement in alpha wave patterns

In a study with healthy seniors, using Herzion for 8 weeks let to a significant increase in alpha wave activity as measured by EEG. This change is linked to potential benefits such as reduced stress, enhanced concentration, and improved mental clarity.

02

Attention and Concentration Improvement

A significant improvement in CERAD-K attention scores indicates overall cognitive enhancements in areas such as focus, processing speed, and working memory. These improvements may contribute to reduced mental fatigue and increased efficiency in daily activities,learning, and work—ultimately supporting better quality of life and well-being.

03

Reduction in depression scale scores

The CES-D (Center for Epidemiologic Studies Depression Scale) is a self-report depression screening tool developed by the U.S. National Institute of Mental Health (NIMH). It has been widely used in epidemiological studies on depression in community populations. In a study with healthy older adults, the use of the Herzion device showed an approximate 33% reduction in CES-D scores, suggesting a potential positive effect on emotional stability and depressive symptom reduction in the elderly.

What is CERAD-K?

The CERAD-K test is a standardized Korean-language cognitive assessment tool used for the diagnosis and evaluation of dementia. It is widely utilized in clinical settings, including neurology departments, to assess treatment efficacy by comparing cognitive function before and after intervention.
