How It Was Developed

Herzion is a low-frequency vibroacoustic device, originally based on foundational technology from a 2012 study, and has since been refined to meet modern wellness needs. Developed around the scientific potential of low-frequency vibration stimulation to promote neuronal differentiation and aid in the recovery of damaged neural tissue, Herzion uses non-invasive brain stimulation technology. It is designed for everyday use to support cognitive health and emotional well-being.
